This page is the user interface of the Token Distributor Software (The Front-End).

The Back-End is intended to be used solely by one user at once, as it lacks of multi account logic.

Due of the nature of the Block-Chain, all the operations are irreversible.

Guideline to Token Distribution

The whole process consists in two steps: snapshoting and distribution. Snapshot consists in a scan of the whole BlockChain looking for balances of a particular token. Distribution is the action of send a specific token to the holders resulting of the previus step (snapshot).
